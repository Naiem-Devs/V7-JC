About Me

True and absolute passion was demonstrated to me when my Grandfather visited my family for the first time in America. The way even in his old age he was absolutely devoted by his craft, even while facing the adversities that come with age, inspired me to approach all work I do with an unrelenting fervor. My mom and grandfather both are practicing artists so in a way I feel like a career in Design was a natural choice. Growing up I had the privilege of following my mother show to show being exposed to gallery artists and their thought provoking installations. Early in my life I grew an appreciation for the arts and began asking myself questions about why pieces of art would connect and resonate with me. Very quickly, before I even knew it, I became an intentional designer. I would spend hours a day developing fantasy campaigns for my friends in MS paint and even more hours putting together comics. Very similarly to these early creations driven by childlike wonder, ux design to me is an outlet to flex my creative muscle in such a way that solves problems and connects dots.
