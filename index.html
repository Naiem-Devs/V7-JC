SACH DUBEY

UI/UX DESIGNER
Hi, My Name Is Sach Dubey!

I am a recent graduate from University of California, Davis and am passionate about how design can be used as a form of communication. In the past I have worked for multiple organizations to compose effective UI/UX solutions, and have even piloted a cohort program to benefit small businesses in Davis struggling with formatting effective websites. In my free time I love expressing myself through art, designing clothes, and exploring nature alongside friends. If you want to learn more about my love for design please visit my about me page.
